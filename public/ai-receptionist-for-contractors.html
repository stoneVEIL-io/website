01 / Thesis

Speed to lead is the whole game.

Companies that reach a lead within one hour are nearly 7 times more likely to qualify it. 78% of customers hire whoever responds first. For most contractors, one recovered bid covers the build.

The AI receptionist is the backstop for what falls through the cracks when your office manager is on another line, at lunch, or it is after hours. Not a replacement. A safety net.

02 / Delta vs. answering service

Not a call centre.

No call centre, no per-call fee, no offshore rep reading a script. It runs on your business number, writes in your voice with your trade's language, and hands the qualified lead straight to you.
