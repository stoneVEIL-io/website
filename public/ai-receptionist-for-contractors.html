AI receptionist for contractors

stoneVEIL's AI receptionist answers every call your crew can't. The moment a call goes unanswered, the caller gets a text from your real business number asking what they need — the lead lands in your inbox with a name, number, and job description in seconds, not whenever someone finally checks voicemail.

Does this replace my office manager?

No — it catches what falls through the cracks when your office manager is on another line, at lunch, or it's after hours. Think of it as a backstop, not a replacement.

Companies that reach a lead within one hour are nearly 7× more likely to qualify it (Harvard Business Review), and 78% of customers hire whoever responds first. For most contractors, one recovered bid covers the build.

How is this different from a call centre or per-call answering service?

No call centre, no per-call fee, no offshore rep reading a script. It runs on your business number, writes in your voice with your trade's language, and hands the qualified lead straight to you. You own the templates and can turn any of it off in one click.
