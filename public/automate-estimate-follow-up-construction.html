Why Day 2, Day 5, Day 10 specifically.

It mirrors how long a homeowner or GC typically sits on a quote before deciding. Early enough to stay top of mind, spaced enough to not feel pushy.

Quoted jobs that never get a follow-up call go cold and get lost to whoever calls back first. This is the layer that stops that from being on your calendar.

What makes the cadence stop.

Any customer reply halts the sequence immediately, whether it lands as a text, an email, or a call. This is follow-up, not spam. Nobody gets a robotic double-text.
