Automate estimate follow-up for construction

Quoted jobs that never get a follow-up call go cold and get lost to whoever calls back first. stoneVEIL auto-chases unsent bids by text and email on a Day 2 / Day 5 / Day 10 cadence — and stops instantly the moment the customer replies, so nobody gets a robotic double-text.

What makes the cadence stop?

Any reply from the customer — a text, an email, a call — halts the sequence immediately. This is follow-up, not spam.

Why Day 2 / Day 5 / Day 10 specifically?

It mirrors how long a homeowner or GC typically sits on a quote before deciding — early enough to stay top-of-mind, spaced enough to not feel pushy.

Day 2 A quick nudge — short, friendly, offers to answer questions.
Day 5 A check-in with a specific next step (call, revised scope, timeline).
Day 10 A last-call, no-guilt message that closes the loop either way.
