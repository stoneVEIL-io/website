Automate scheduling and invoicing for contractors

Booked jobs and unpaid invoices both need chasing, and both usually lose to whatever's on fire that day. stoneVEIL automates appointment reminders so fewer no-shows slip through, and chases unpaid invoices on a 7 / 14 / 30-day cadence that auto-pauses the moment a customer responds or pays.

Does this replace my accounting software?

No — it layers reminders and chase sequences on top of the scheduling and invoicing tools you already use. Nothing to migrate.

What stops it from nagging a customer who already paid?

The sequence auto-pauses on any customer reply or on payment confirmation from your existing invoicing tool. No double-chasing.

Day 7 A friendly first reminder with a payment link and the invoice attached.
Day 14 A firmer check-in that offers a phone number to call if there's a question.
Day 30 A final notice before the invoice moves to a manual follow-up on your end.
